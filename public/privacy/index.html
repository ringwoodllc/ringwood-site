Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Ringwood Field Ops is a private work tool for Ringwood and the clients it serves. It is used by invited staff and client accounts to log tickets, track equipment and service, and manage inventory. This policy explains what the app collects, why, and how it is handled. If you have a question, write to hello@ringwood.ai.

Who the app is for

This is not a consumer app. Accounts are created by Ringwood for staff and authorized client users. You sign in to use it. There is no public sign-up and no advertising.

What we collect

  • Account details. The email address or username and the role assigned to your account, so we can sign you in and show you the right work.
  • Work you enter. Tickets, equipment and asset records, service history, inventory counts, notes, and the client a record belongs to.
  • Photos and files. Pictures you take or upload (for example equipment nameplates, order sheets, and documents) and any files you attach.
  • Basic technical data. Standard request information such as the time of a request and the kind of device or browser, used to keep the service running and secure.

We do not ask for or collect location beyond what you choose to type into a record. We do not use third party advertising or tracking.

How we use it

  • To provide the app: to sign you in, save your work, and show records to the people allowed to see them.
  • To read photos you upload. When you scan a document or an order sheet, the image is sent to our processing provider so the app can read the text and fill in the record. You stay in control of what is saved.
  • To keep the service working and secure, and to fix problems.

How information is shared

We do not sell personal information, and we do not share it for advertising. Information is handled by the providers that run the app on our behalf:

  • Hosting and database. Records and photos are stored with our cloud hosting and database providers (Cloudflare and Supabase).
  • Photo and document reading. Images you choose to scan are sent to Anthropic to read their contents back into the app. They are processed to return that result and are not used to train models.

We may also disclose information if the law requires it, or to protect the safety and rights of Ringwood, its clients, and its users.

How long we keep it

Work records are kept for as long as the account and client relationship are active, because they are the operating history of the space (for example tickets and service records). When an account or client is removed, related logins are removed and records are unlinked. You can ask us to delete your account.

Security

Access requires a login, and what each account can see is limited to its role and client. Secrets and tokens are held server side and are never exposed in the app. No system is perfect, but we work to protect the information in our care.

Children

The app is a workplace tool and is not directed to children. It is not intended for anyone under 16.

Your choices

You can ask to see, correct, or delete the information tied to your account. To make a request, or to ask anything about this policy, write to hello@ringwood.ai.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change means the updated policy applies.
